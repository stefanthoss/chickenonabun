The Hideout

Rating? 5 out of 10

What to order on the side? Truffle Fries

Where? 9051 Soquel Dr, Aptos, CA 95003

Links? Website | Foursquare

Review published on September 18, 2021 by COAB Team.

The Hideout is a small American restaurant in the woods of Aptos. In the Sandwich & Burger section of their menu is a fried chicken sandwich. It’s a basic sandwich with slaw, pickle slices, a chipotle aioli. While the potato bun was pretty good, the chicken piece was disappointing - small and not very crispy. Overall, the sandwich was slightly on the dry side and not particularly memorable.

They have fantastic truffle fries with parmesan though, so get some of that and pair it with their spicy aioli or some ranch dressing.
